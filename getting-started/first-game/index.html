    Your First Game¶

    A detailed walkthrough of playing your first SuperMaze game.

    Before You Start¶

    Make sure you have: - SuperMaze open in your browser - Entered a username and logged in - Selected your game mode (Singleplayer or Multiplayer)

    Game Screen Overview¶

    When you start a game, you'll see:

    • Maze Grid: The game area with corridors and walls
    • Red Cell: Your player position (starting point)
    • Green Cell: The goal/end point
    • Gray Walls: Obstacles you cannot pass through
    • White Passages: Open corridors you can move through
    • Stopwatch: Time counter in the top-right
    • Keystrokes Display: Shows which keys you're pressing (bottom-left)

    Starting to Play¶

    Step 1: Understand the Layout¶

    Take a moment to observe the maze: - Locate your position (red cell) - Find the goal (green cell) - Identify nearby passages and walls

    Step 2: First Movement¶

    Press W or Up Arrow to move forward. You should move one cell in that direction.

    Step 3: Navigate to the Goal¶

    Use the movement controls to navigate: - Move cautiously to explore the maze - Turn left and right to find open paths - Avoid dead ends when possible - Work toward the green goal cell

    Step 4: Complete the Maze¶

    When you reach the green cell, the game automatically: 1. Stops your timer 2. Records your completion time 3. Awards you 50 XP 4. Saves the run to your leaderboard 5. Shows a victory screen

    Victory Screen¶

    After completing a maze, you'll see:

    • Your completion time
    • Option to "Replay Run" - watch your recorded moves
    • Option to "Return to Menu" - play another maze

    Understanding Your Performance¶

    Time Measurement¶

    Time is displayed in MM:SS.MS format: - MM = Minutes - SS = Seconds - MS = Milliseconds

    Example: 02:35.450 = 2 minutes, 35 seconds, 450 milliseconds

    Time Benchmarks¶

    • Expert: Under 2 minutes
    • Advanced: 2-4 minutes
    • Intermediate: 4-6 minutes
    • Beginner: 6+ minutes

    Don't worry about speed on your first game - focus on learning the controls!

    Tips for Your First Game¶

    1. Use Systematic Exploration: Try moving in one direction until you hit a wall, then try another direction
    2. Map Mentally: Remember sections of the maze you've explored
    3. Avoid Backtracking: Try not to return to areas you've already been
    4. Stay Calm: Take your time - speed comes with practice
    5. Watch the Stopwatch: Use it to track your progress

    Common Mistakes¶

    Mistake 1: Panic in Dead Ends¶

    What to do: Calmly backtrack and try a different path. You can move in any direction with arrows/WASD.

    Mistake 2: Losing Orientation¶

    What to do: Stop moving and look at the maze structure. The green goal cell is always visible.

    Mistake 3: Trying to Climb Walls¶

    What to do: Walls are impassable. You must find corridors around them.

    Mistake 4: Using Wrong Controls¶

    What to do: Remember: WASD = arrow keys. Only these 4 inputs work. Make sure you're not typing elsewhere.

    After Your First Game¶

    View Your Results¶

    1. After victory, click "Return to Menu"
    2. Click "Leaderboard" or "Saved Replays"
    3. Your run appears in the list with:
    4. Username
    5. Completion time
    6. Date played

    Watch Your Replay¶

    1. Open Leaderboard or Saved Replays
    2. Click on your run
    3. Watch your moves play back automatically

    Improve Your Time¶

    • Play more mazes to learn patterns
    • Challenge yourself on harder difficulties
    • Adjust graphics for better visibility
    • Compare times with previous runs

    Common Questions¶

    Q: Can I pause the game? - A: No, but you can close the browser tab and resume later (your progress in-maze is saved)

    Q: What happens if I click "Exit"? - A: Your current run is abandoned and you return to the menu. Progress is not saved.

    Q: Can I play the same maze twice? - A: No, a new maze is generated each time. Mazes are always unique.

    Q: Why is my time so slow? - A: This is normal for your first game! Speed improves with practice.

    Q: How do I replay a specific run? - A: Open the Leaderboard, find the run, and click on it to watch the replay.

    Congratulations on completing your first SuperMaze game! Ready for a new challenge?
