    Quick Start¶

    Get up and running with SuperMaze in just a few minutes.

    Starting the Game¶

    Singleplayer¶

    1. Open src/web/index.html in your browser
    2. You'll see the login screen
    3. Enter your username
    4. Click "Continue"
    5. Click "Singleplayer"
    6. Start playing!

    Multiplayer¶

    1. Start the server: cd src/server && npm start
    2. Open http://localhost:3000 in your browser
    3. Enter your username
    4. Click "Continue"
    5. Click "Singleplayer" or join multiplayer mode
    6. Start playing!

    Game Controls¶

    Use these controls to move through the maze:

    Key Action
    W or Up Arrow Move up
    A or Left Arrow Move left
    S or Down Arrow Move down
    D or Right Arrow Move right

    Your First Maze¶

    1. You start at the red cell (top-left area)
    2. Navigate through the white passages
    3. Avoid walls (dark gray)
    4. Reach the green cell (end) to complete the maze
    5. Your time is recorded automatically
    6. View your run on the leaderboard

    Main Menu Buttons¶

    • Singleplayer: Start a new maze game
    • Saved Replays: View past runs and watch replays
    • Theme Dropdown: Change the visual theme
    • Customize (+): Create custom themes with colors and images
    • Leaderboard: View all your completed runs
    • Settings: Adjust difficulty, graphics, and advanced options
    • Docs: Open this documentation
    • Logout: Sign out and switch accounts

    Settings¶

    Click the Settings button (⚙️) to customize:

    • Difficulty: Easy, Medium, or Hard
    • Graphics: Low, Mid, High, or Advanced
    • Custom Settings: Edit JSON directly

    Tips for Playing¶

    1. Learn the Controls: Practice moving smoothly through corridors
    2. Explore Slowly: Take time to map out the maze mentally
    3. Watch Your Time: Challenge yourself to complete faster runs
    4. Replay Runs: Click runs in the leaderboard to watch replays
    5. Customize Your Experience: Adjust difficulty and graphics for your preference

    Understanding Your Stats¶

    • Level: Based on total XP (100 XP per level)
    • XP: Earn 50 XP per completed maze
    • Time: Your completion time for each run
    • Leaderboard: Shows your best runs sorted by time

    Keyboard Shortcuts¶

    Shortcut Action
    WASD or Arrows Move in maze
    Esc Not implemented (use Exit button)

    Troubleshooting¶

    Q: The maze looks small/large - A: Adjust graphics mode in Settings

    Q: My scores aren't saving - A: Check browser localStorage is enabled (Settings > Privacy)

    Q: Can't move in the maze - A: Ensure you're not typing in an input field - A: Try clicking the maze area first

    Q: Want to change username - A: Click the Logout button, then enter a new username
